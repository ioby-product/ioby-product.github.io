19:T597,<p>The 5 Points Business District aka 5 Points Merchants Group (5 PM Group)was formed in 2017.  The organization consists of the business owners and two residential representatives of Observatory Hill.  With empty and boarded-up storefronts in our business corridor, the group focus was to change its negative perception of Observatory Hill becoming a community on the decline by bringing back vitality to the business corridor.
