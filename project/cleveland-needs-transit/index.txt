18:T66d,<p><span>Good transit is essential for members of our community to access jobs, school, healthcare... opportunity. </span></p>
<p>Transit helps to reduce traffic, pollution, and oil consumption, all of which benefit both riders and non-riders alike.</p>
<p>And it's more than just Cleveland. Good transit helps our whole region: it provides access to jobs, creates jobs, revitalizes neighborhoods, and saves money on infrastructure and vehicle costs.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Transit in our community is in a death spiral. Fares are too expensive, service has been cut drastically, buses are late and often don't get people where they need to go in a reasonable amount of time. What is to be done?</p>
<p><strong>Clevelanders for Public Transit (CPT) exists to make transit better- by riders, for riders.</strong></p>
<p>Clevelanders for Public Transit was born in 2015 when transit riders in our community faced more fare increases and cuts to transit service. <span>Since 2005, transit fares have doubled while service has declined over 25%. </span><span>Fares were planned to increase to $2.75 which would have made fares as high as New York City. Of course transit service in our community is no where near NYC. </span></p>
<p><span>CPT organized to prevent this fare increase, saving transit riders over $1 million every year! We also organized residents of Lakeview Terrance, East 79th Street, and Cedar Avenue to maintain transit for those that need it most. </span></p>
<div>
	<span>All this was done with the hard work of volunteers. <strong>Now we need your help to continue to make transit better. </strong></span></div>
<p> </p>
19:T547,<p>Clevelanders for Public Transit is known in Cleveland as being the best organized group. We are committed, we have a vision, and we do the work.</p>
<p>We are working towards the full implementation of our Transit Bill of Rights that was developed with riders in 2015.</p>
<p><a href="http://clefortransit.org/transit-bill-of-rights/">Read our Transit Bill of Rights here.</a></p>
<div>
