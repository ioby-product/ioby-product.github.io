<div>
	Fortunately, we are not starting from square one. The Northeast Minneapolis Tool Library (NEMTL) opened a little over one year ago and already has over 400 members. NEMTL is helping us navigate the logistics of opening and running a tool library. The Saint Paul Tool Library will actually be a branch of NEMTL, which allows us to use their non-profit status (making your donation tax deductible), share their insurance policy, and learn from their mistakes and successes on how to run a tool library.</div>
