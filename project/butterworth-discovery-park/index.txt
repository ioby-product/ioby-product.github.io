<p>The Butterworth Discovery Park is sure to be the best, new thing in our small town. Just close your eyes and see the round, center cut logs as stepping stones or large, leafy arches to provide shade and flattened logs laying down used as balance beams. Now don't you wish you lived closer to bring your children or grandchildren to this marvelous park?  A Healthy Clendenin will come together with the people of the community to raise funds and build the park as one combined force.
