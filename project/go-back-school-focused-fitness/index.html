Go Back to School Focused on Fitness!

GYM STAR stands for Great Young Minds Striving Toward Amazing Results. Send students back to school in the GYM STAR sneakers as a reminder that we are all GYM STARS.

Leader

#CleanUpBrownsville Brandi The Great

Location

61 Glenmore Ave Brownsville, NY 11212

About the project

In September 2018 GYM STAR RECREATION began an AM fitness program that inspired students to come to school early and helped them stay focused throughout the day.

The students were attracted to the apparel that the GYM STAR RECREATION fitness instructors wore and asked how they can help to make the apparel available for them to purchase. 

In February 2019 these students organized a week-long pop-up shop during their mid-winter break to fundraise and #CleanUpBrownsville was born.

The money raised was invested into creating a prototype for a sneaker, and the Young Leaders of #CleanUpBrownsville planned a Last Day of School Fitness Party ™️ to celebrate their release and kick off their fundraising campaign. They have already raised $300, and look forward to partnering with IOBY to receive matched funds to reach their goal.

This project will give students the opportunity to fundraise to earn a pair of GYM STAR Level 1sneakers and other GYM STAR gear at no cost.

The Steps

This project will begin on July 20th to kick off fundraising efforts and provide students and members of the community the opportunity to get involved. 

Once the funds are raised, they will be used to manufacture the sneakers and provide them as an incentive to reward students who successfully complete a 10-week fitness program during the school year. This program will encourage students to arrive to school early and start their day focused on fitness. The 10-week program will show participating students how to organize their energy in a positive way through fitness and transform them into fitness influencers for their classmates. 

 

Why we‘re doing it

The Young Leaders of #CleanUpBrownsville express that participating in a fitness program before the start of the school day helped them to stay focused and perform better during the day, in addition to helping them to lose weight and be healthier. 

Our goal is to spread this program to schools across Brownsville and to use the GYM STAR Level 1 sneaker and other gear as an incentive to reward students who successfully complete a 10-week fitness program. We want to GYM'POWER™️ these students to be fitness influencers for their classmates to help create a school culture that is focused on fitness!
