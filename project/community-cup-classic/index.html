The Community Cup Classic™ is an annual county wide family engagement and wellness competition.

The mission of the Community Cup Classic™ is to:

  • Connect youth and families to a network of evidence based supports to reduce health disparities and be successful in today’s global economy.
  • Increase positive adult engagement by creating opportunities where youth are celebrated.

  • Raise one million dollars to be distributed annually for sustainable youth development opportunities and to eliminate barriers in becoming life long learners.

The Steps

Through direct partnerships community residents will have an opportunity to work with leaders and professionals throughout the year who will provide mentorship in leadership, opportunities to participate in civic engagement, career training, academics, peer support and health/wellness and fitness activities.  

 

 

Why we‘re doing it

As positive adult engagement increases, our strategies instill in students the self-confidence and motivation to stay in school, graduate from high school and pursue post-high school education, career training and/or employment.  The fundraising model provides sustainable resources to support the scalability of strategy.
