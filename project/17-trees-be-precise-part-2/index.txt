18:T4cd,<p>Join the team creating a crowd-funded children’s book (for ages 3-7yrs) about the value of community activism! Engaged citizenship strengthens communities and builds trust and resilience, grounding attributes in turbulent political and ecological times. </p>

<p>This children's book offers a starting point to talk with kids about community involvement and how ANYBODY can be the spark in their neighborhood. It also outlines the benefits trees provide, and offers tips on how to get started organizing in your own neighborhood.  A generous grant from Partnerships for Parks kicked off production in 2021, resulting in the first exciting illustrations by NYC artist Juliette Borda. We'll be dropping those here in the coming weeks.</p>

<p>Drawing on the true experience of the Proyecto de Embellecimiento 137th Street Beautification Project, which celebrated its 10th Anniversary in 2020, 1<em>7 Trees, To Be Precise</em> aims to inspire the newest generation of engaged citizens.</p>

<p>This second campaign will fund the remaining illustrations and the printing of 1,000 books to be distributed for free to community leaders. Please see budget tab for a more detailed breakdown.</p>
