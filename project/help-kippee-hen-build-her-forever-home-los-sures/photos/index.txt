17:T593,<p>In June 2017 I began fostering an orphan Rhode Island Red hen named Kippee. Since that time, I've grown quite attached to the very sweet, personable hen and it's been my mission to get her a coop in the local neighborhood so she could have a proper home, yet I could still care for her.</p>
<p>A member from GreenThumbNYC tipped me off to a wonderful organization named <a href="http://www.southsideunitedhdfc.org/" target="_blank">Los Sures</a>, which is a community-based, non-profit organization that has served as an integral part in rebuilding the South Side of Williamsburg, Brooklyn since 1972. Los Sures has undertaken large-scale rehabilitation of many buildings, allowing families and individuals to live comfortably and creating a safe and sustainable neighborhood. Additionally, the organization provides a multitude of much-needed services, including a senior center, food pantry and food independence operations, including the build out and management of local gardens, a hydroponic program, and of course—a hen coop equipped with fresh eggs!</p>
<p>However, the hen coop and run has never been properly designed, it needs proper winterization, and also the area needs to be "rat-proofed". We want to see Kippee and her friends have a fabulous, well-working coop and run; as well as give the community members the chicken coop area they always dreamt of, while engaging them in the build out process!</p>
