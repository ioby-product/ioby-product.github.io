19:T11f1,<p><strong>Groundation: Trickle Up Habitat presents</strong><br />
	<strong><em>The Most Fabulous Green Wall Ever Built</em></strong></p>
<p>Imagine -</p>
<p>An undulating green wall that will literally <em>pour</em> light onto dim sidewalks, throughout multi-colored native plantings, and create an attractive and calming setting with the year-round sound of water falling through a sculpted downspout.  </p>
<p>No, no, this is not some crazy artist's dream. Rather, this is <em>The Most Fabulous Green Wall Ever Built</em> and is being produced <em>right now</em> in the heart of West Oakland by Groundation: Trickle up Habitat - a new partnership between artists, environmentalists, and the city of Oakland.</p>
<p>Groundation: Trickle Up Habitat is a blooming partnership of<a href="http://www.whollyh2o.org/"> Wholly H2O</a>,<a href="http://www.americansteelstudios.com/"> American Steel Studios</a> (ASS), Green Wall expert Nick Gardner, <a href="http://www.indradesigns.com/">Indra Designs</a> and the<a href="http://www2.oaklandnet.com/Government/o/PWA/o/EC/s/CreeksandWatershed/OAK025822"> City of Oakland’s Rain Barrel Program</a> (ORBP). Each partner brings a unique element to this glistening green wall project - ASS the location, lighting and water sculpture; Nick Gardner and Indra Designs the wall, water system; Wholly H2O the partner and volunteer coordination, educational materials and community outreach; ORBP two large rainwater harvesting cisterns. It’s an amazing team loaded with capacity and experience.  </p>
<p>We are taking existing green wall design to a new level by building a sculpturally designed vertical green wall. <em>The Most Fabulous Greenwall Ever Built</em> is being constructed with recycled materials organic soils and native plants, to be fed by a solar-powered 5650 gallon capacity rainwater harvesting system.</p>
<p>Now all we need are the funds to continue to trickle up our habitat from the ground up and from the sky down.</p>
<p><strong>How You Can Help</strong></p>
<p>We are already rolling but we need funds to cover some of the expenses of this outstanding green wall project. Be a part of it by contributing $10 or more.  We especially like triple digits!</p>
<p><strong>More About Who We Are</strong></p>
<p>American Steel Studios is an important anchor on the currently revitalizing corridor of<a href="http://structurehub.com/blog/2009/10/twenty-years-after-loma-pieta-earthquake-mandela-parkway-finally-improving-oakland-street-life-just-a-bit/"> Mandela Parkway</a>’s recycled water-fed greenway, running close to the<a href="http://www.portofoakland.com/"> Port of Oakland</a> and the<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Oakland_(BART_station)"> West Oakland Bart statio</a>n. Building on American Steel Studio’s track record as a center for art and community gatherings,** this artfully-designed green wall will boost the local economy by creating a beautiful atmosphere to boost the attraction of high-density housing, restaurants, local businesses, pop-up markets. That brings new employment opportunities and needed services to our community.</p>
<p>Our green wall will tie into the newly revitalized, recycled water-fed greenway running in front of ASS, as well as the new restaurants, and pop-up markets taking root within two blocks. The Most Fabulous Green Wall Ever Built fits right into the City of Oakland’s West Oakland redevelopment goals.</p>
<p>We’re doubly excited because The Most Fabulous Green Wall Ever Built fits right into the City of Oakland’s <a href="http://www2.oaklandnet.com/Government/o/CityAdministration/d/NeighborhoodInvestment/o/WestOakland/index.htm">redevelopment goals</a> for our neighborhood. Our green wall  accomplish graffiti abatement, <a href="http://www.phillygreenwall.com/media/benefits/IndoorAirQuality.pdf">improve air quality</a>, an<a href="http://www.arb.ca.gov/ch/communities/ra/westoakland/westoakland.htm"> ongoing issue</a> in West Oakland, and <a href="http://www.environmentalleader.com/2009/09/22/green-wall-lowers-temperature-25/">reduce energy consumption</a> by regulating temperatures inside the American Steel building. This substantial green wall soften the hard environment, acting as a tonic to ease stress and fatigue and provide a much-needed connection to nature, missing in the modern concrete jungle of West Oakland. Moreover, we will have an ongoing demonstration to use for teaching purposes.</p>
<p>Art, habitat supporting local biodiversity, rainwater harvesting, faling water and solar power. Be part of it!  </p>
