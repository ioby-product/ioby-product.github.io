<p>ORIGINAL BUDGET</p>

<p>This budget was discussed and approved in our community meetings, which are open to the public and are welcome to participation from anyone who is a recipient of our services or a volunteer on our projects. We discuss and vote on additional spending projects at these meetings, which currently wouldn't exceed the $2000 per month budgeted for "emergency &amp; seasonal needs", unless a serious emergency were to occur, in which case we would adopt a new budget. </p>

<p> </p>

<p>We're hoping to raise money to keep our neighbors safe and fed. The funds raised here will be redistributed among community efforts that are delivering fresh and nutritious food to folx, ensuring neighbors have basic sanitation supplies and delivering family care items (i.e. diapers and wipes) to neighbors in need. Based on the requests we receive, the AMAN community will collaborate to determine the best way to distribute funds with the majority of them going to local food relief efforts that are providing immediate relief to individuals experiencing food insecurity as a result of the COVID-19 Pandemic.  </p>
