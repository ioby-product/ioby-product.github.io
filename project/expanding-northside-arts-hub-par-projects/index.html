What's the hype about?

Located along Hoffner Street — a stone's throw from Gulow Street and a skip and a hop from Happen's Garden — we're doing something big. Over three quarters of an acre BIG! ... After over two years of design & redesign, in March of 2021 all six of our zoning variances were approved and we were given the green light to create our outdoor movie theater & performance space at PAR-Projects. And in July of 2021, we closed on the purchase of 15,000 square feet of EXPANSION space, Studeō PAR- ...a creative space created to welcome and support like-minded creative organizations!

Slowly and steadily, year after year, we've been building up to this point. So even if you're unable to contribute to the project or join us for a tour, we do wonder if you'll take a few minutes to flip through our campaign brochure. Still, we're seeking support to help US keep this community-building project moving forward. So please do read on.

 

A few more details?

Within the walls of each of our gallery spaces, one of our primary goals is to provide deep-seated support for ambitious and underrepresented artists. And if you've been to any of our exhibitions over the years, you've more than likely seen this first hand. We also take every chance we can to help our surrounding community to shine, creatively. The latter is what's driving this project.

Our campus addresses desires of both artists and the community by aligning with a few areas of need identified by our neighbors, including:

  • A venue for outdoor movies and performances
  • A place to learn various art forms from more than one organization
  • A family friendly location for events & activations
  • More outdoor community gathering spaces
  • Flexible and affordable studio spaces
  • Space for likeminded nonprofits

Why we‘re doing it

We believe in being a part of the community where we are located. Our director lives here, in Northside, as do members from our board. And as we contiune growing with the community, we will continue to identify place where we can offer our support, much like we do by organizing events like Northside Summer Market.

Now that we've successfully navigated our zoning challenges, it's time to start focussing our efforts on creating a centralized arts hub -- one that caters to our community, yet helps establish our community as a regional arts destination.

Our first order of business is to create Northside's FIRST outdoor movie theater!
