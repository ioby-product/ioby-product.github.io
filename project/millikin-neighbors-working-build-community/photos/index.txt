17:T727,<p>We have been working diligently for the past 2 1/2 years to bring our community together, where everyone is included and made to feel like a partner. Since the outbreak of the Covid-19 pandemic, we have seen an increase in daily use of this public space which has given us the opportunity to get to know more and more of our neighbors and include them in our efforts and activities. Obviously many of our plans for this summer had to be sidelined, but that has not stopped us from working together on shared goals for the community. Earlier in the spring, we asked our neighbors what they most wanted to see at the playground and the resounding response was 'a slide'. Starting with a group of 6 - 10 neighbors, we have grown to over 100 members actively engaged in these efforts. </p>
