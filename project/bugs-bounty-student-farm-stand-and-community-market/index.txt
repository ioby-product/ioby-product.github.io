18:T701,<p><span><span>BUGS is supporting its 6th grade class to launch a Student Farm</span><span> Stand and Community Market. This end-of-semester project is designed to increase awareness about food justice, support locally sourced foods, and encourage gardening both within the BUGS community and across the schools (2 Pre-K programs and a public middle school) that also share our building. During the spring semester students will also engage in related community service projects such as finding new uses for unwanted books, sharing original writings about the market and the impact of herbs and spices on different cultures with Pre-K students, and creating functional art projects for the market made out of recycled goods.</span></span></p>
<p> </p>

The project is in collaboration with </span><span>Slow Food </span><span>and </span><span>in partnership with a local farmer with the NYC GreenMarket in Prospect Park</span><span>. Our goal is to increase community awareness of social, economic, and environmental sustainability and encourage more environmentally friendly actions and behavior.
