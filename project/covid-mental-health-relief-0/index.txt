18:T4f5,<p>The Weissman Children's Foundation’s <strong>Mental Health Relief Initiative</strong> is dedicated to providing full access to evidence-based mental health treatments <em>for every child and family in need</em>.</p>

<p><strong>The Problem</strong></p>

<p>There is a mental health crisis in this country. And it is largely ignored by the federal government and insurance companies.</p>

<p>With COVID, civil unrest and economic hardship, it’s not surprising to see spiking rates of anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, child behavior issues and marital/family conflict.</p>

<p>But this is all much worse for our nation’s most vulnerable and marginalized families, too frequently <em>denied access to affordable, effective mental healthcare</em>.</p>

<p><strong>The Solution</strong></p>

<p>The Weissman Children’s Foundation aims to bridge this gap by providing effective and immediate mental healthcare for all children, families and young adults, <em>regardless of their ability to pay</em>. </p>

<p>Our Mental Health Relief Initiative offers highly specialized programs and services for the <em>uninsured and underinsured, </em>with an emphasis on helping <em>low-income, Black, Indigenous and People of Color populations.</em> </p>
