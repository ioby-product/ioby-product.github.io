<p>Norwalk Mutual Aid/Ayuda Mutua started as a way to build community connections on a peer-to-peer basis, for individuals to help each other get their needs met during the COVID-19 crisis. We've already helped over 120 households through our radically inclusive network of neighbors helping neighbors, but the need for food assistance has grown far beyond our capacity to help through individual shopping trips.</p>

<p>This project is the beginning of an effort to grow our current small-scale food collection and distribution program. We need funds to purchase supplies for making deliveries, cleaning and organizing our collection site, and keeping our volunteers safe.
