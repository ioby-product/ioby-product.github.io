<p>FROM APRIL 17, 2020</p>

<p>Since we've all hunkered down in our homes the past few weeks, we haven't had the chance to say hello to our elder neighbors at Riverview Terrace on West 25th!  We know they are trying to stay safe at home, and we want to send them all a care package to let them know we're thinking of them! <br />
We're purchasing most of these items from local Cleveland businesses, and hope to get the care packages distributed as soon as possible. We know it's not a lot, but we want our neighbors to know that we care very much and are here to help them get through to better days!<br />
<br />
