How It Worked

You're the experts on your neighborhood, we were the experts on crowdfunding.Our crowdfunding platform connected you with support and funding from your community. With ioby, you wouldn't just put up a page, you'd work with our team to craft the most effective fundraising campaign no matter how big or small your goal was.Read more about our services below.

Fees

At the end of your campaign, we retain a 4% processing fee that pays our secure credit card processor and an optional 8% fee that is only retained if you opt for ioby's fiscal sponsorship service.

Criteria

Read up on the handful of eligibility criteria we have as a 501c3 nonprofit.

Fiscal Sponsorship

Fiscal sponsorship allows you yo accept tax-deductible donations if you are an individual or working with an unincorporated group.

Coaching

A coach will work with you to reach your goal, whether that's $500, $5,000, or $50,000—we're here for you!

Match Funding

We partner with funders from across the United States to double, triple, and even quadruple donations made to your project page. To learn about current opportunities, head over to our match programs page.
